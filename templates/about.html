About Project

The Sugar Cane Yield Prediction project aims to harness data-driven insights to forecast sugar cane production yields accurately. Given the significance of sugar cane as a primary agricultural commodity in industries like sugar production, biofuels, and other energy sources, predicting yield can be essential for optimizing agricultural practices, resource management, and overall industry sustainability.
